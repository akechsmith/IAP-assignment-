Awards and Distinctions

Dr. John Garang has not received any major award such as the Noble Peace Prize. But he will be remebered as the man who brought peace to Sudan.

He will also be remembered as:

  • John Garang will be remembered as a man who fought for twenty-two years for the liberation of South Sudan
  • John Garang will be remembered as the liberator of the marginalised people of East, South, North and West Sudan.
  • John Garang will be remembered as a man with a vision for a New Sudan of equality, justice, secularisation, and freedom of worship
  • John Garang will be remembered as the man who brought peace to Sudan through the Comprehensive Peace Agreement that ended the war between North and South Sudan, which killed three million people and displaced over six million Sudanese.
